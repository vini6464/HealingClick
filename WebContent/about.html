About

Healing click is a web based platform that strives to redefine the healthcare service industry through an integrative web based platform, which allows patients, physicians and pharmacists to interact on a real time basis, thereby eliminating time delays that the client faces in the real world.

Our platform strives to provide a user friendly platform for physicians, patients and pharmacies to interact, it provides them the following features:

  • Physicians
  • Patients
  • Pharmacies

Doctors -

Virtual Assistant:

With the aid of a customised digital calendar, physicians are enabled to track their appointments effectively and receive timely reminders of the various appointments during the day.

Online Payment Integration:

We provide a hassle free online payment platform in order to facilitate the collection and transfer of the physician’s fees.

Doctor Profiles:

An online profile aids physicians to cater to the needs of patients on a real time basis without the need for either party to be physically present. This feature supports the physician to connect with patients, pharmacists, peers etc. virtually and carry on business with ease.

Live Feeds:

Physicians can receive and share the latest updates in the world of medicine, hold discussions with patients and other physicians. Moreover, the news feed is customised on the basis of the physician’s interest, therefore keeping him/her up to date on various developments across the world.

Notifications:

Physicians receive notifications online or via SMS with regard to any updates from their newsfeed or emergency communication from patients, pharmacies and other parties.

Patients -

Medical Trackers:

Medical trackers facilitate patients to virtually track their medical health, physicians receive timely alerts to monitor the patient’s well-being and commence an early diagnosis if necessary.

Vaccination Tracker:

The vaccination tracker simplifies the task of keeping track of the essential vaccinations that the patient is required to take, This tool can be customised bearing in mind the unique needs and requirements of the client.

Online Pharmacies:

You can now purchase medicines and other healthcare products from the convenience of your home, we connect you to medical stores in your vicinity and assist you in experiencing a flexible and safe shopping experience.

Networking:

Channel the power of our virtual social network and connect with physicians and pharmacists. We enable the client to leverage the power of networking to lead a healthier life.

Appointments:

The struggles to get a doctor’s appointment will become a distant memory, as we at healing click help you view the physician’s calendar and select a date and time of your convenience to fix an appointment.

Chat Support:

You can now utilise our internal messaging platform to communicate with physicians, pharmacies and the support team, who are ever willing to cater to your needs and queries 24/7.

Safer Payments:

We provide a secure virtual payment portal in order to ensure the safety of your payment and payment information.

Notifications and Reminders:

In our busy schedule we often tend to forget important dates, events and appointments, our platform provides you with timely reminders via web notifications, and our innovative SMS reminders, to ensure that you do not miss your treatment, vaccinations, Hospital Visits etc.

Pharmacy -

Networking:

Using our platform pharmacies can connect with physicians, other pharmacists and clients to embark on establishing a wider client base for your products, and accurately assess and understand the needs and requirements of the customer and cater to it more effectively.

Flexibility:

Pharmacies can now gain access to an increased level of flexibility with regard to receiving orders online with a customised discount and a delivery charge option.

Scalability:

We facilitate you to monitor your customer base and market coverage in order to better assess your market demand and accordingly increase or decrease your product supply in order to accommodate the existing market demand.

Marketing:

Healing click’s user base provides pharmacies with a readily available market to expand operations, taking into consideration the feedback from our users, pharmacies can tailor their product placement and supplies in order to provide a better fit to the existing demand for it’s products.

Online Payment Integration:

We provide a secure virtual portal that facilitates pharmacies to transfer and receive funds with increased safety and efficiency.

Notifications and Reminders:

We provide pharmacies with web and SMS notifications with regard to the healthcare product requirements of our users and therefore enable pharmacies to capitalise on the demand by connecting our user base to pharmacies on a real time basis.
